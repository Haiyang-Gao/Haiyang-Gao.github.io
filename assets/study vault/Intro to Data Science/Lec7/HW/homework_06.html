1. Hypothesis test on a single proportion: Was Paul the Octopus psychic?

Paul the Octopus (26 January 2008 26 October 2010), was a common octopus who was suspected of having psychic abilities. To test Pauls psychic abilities, workers at the Sea Life Centre in Oberhausen, Germany, assesed whether Paul could predict the winners of the 2010 World Cup soccer games. Before each soccer game, two containers of food (mussels) were lowered into the octopus tank. The containers were identical, except for country flags of the opposing teams, one on each container. Whichever container Paul opened was deemed his predicted winner.

# write the generate_proportion() function 
def generate_proportion(n, heads_probability):
    rand_nums = np.random.rand(n)
    head_prop = np.sum(rand_nums<heads_probability)/n
    return head_prop
